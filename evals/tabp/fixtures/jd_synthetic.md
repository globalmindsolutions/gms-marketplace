**Company:** Fictional Systems Ltd.

**Location:** Remote (fictional)

## About the Role

Fictional Systems Ltd. is looking for an experienced Senior Backend Engineer
to join the Data Platform team.  You will design and maintain high-throughput
data pipelines, collaborate with cross-functional teams, and mentor junior
engineers.

---

## Must-Have Requirements

These requirements are **essential**; candidates who do not meet them will not
progress regardless of score.

1. **5+ years of professional Python development** — must-have; core
   language for all platform services.
2. **Distributed systems experience** — must-have; candidates must have
   designed or operated systems handling > 1M events/day.
3. **SQL proficiency** — must-have; PostgreSQL or equivalent for 3+
   years in a production setting.
4. **Cloud infrastructure (AWS or GCP)** — must-have; hands-on
   deployment, IAM, and networking in a production cloud account.

---

## Nice-to-Have Requirements

These are preferred but not disqualifying.

5. **Apache Kafka or similar message queue** — preferred; experience
   designing event-driven architectures.
6. **Go language** — nice to have; secondary language used by two
   platform services.
7. **Data engineering tools (dbt, Airflow, or Spark)** — a plus;
   familiarity with orchestration and transformation layers.
8. **Mentoring or tech-lead experience** — preferred; guiding junior
   engineers on architecture and code quality.

---

## Responsibilities

- Design, implement, and operate scalable backend services in Python.
- Collaborate with data science and product teams on pipeline requirements.
- Own reliability and observability of the data ingestion layer.
- Review code, define standards, and mentor two to three junior engineers.

---

## Interview Process

Three-stage process: technical screen, system-design panel, final team interview.
No on-site requirement; all interviews conducted via video call.
